# SecureInsights Hub

Privacy-preserving AI collaboration platform for financial and social inclusion.

## Purpose

Enable banks, insurers, retailers, and public agencies to collaborate on AI-driven analytics without sharing raw customer data. Built for the Snowflake "AI for Good" hackathon.

## Key Goals

- Identify underserved or high-risk populations
- Detect systemic bias in subsidies and financial services
- Improve fraud detection while preserving privacy
- Enable data-driven policy decisions for inclusion and welfare

## Core Value Proposition

Organizations run pre-approved, governed analytics queries across combined datasets, receiving only aggregated insights, risk signals, and AI-generated plain-language explanations.
